About the Artist/Gallery

Biography

Bob Ross (1942-1995) was the ultimate meme master before memes were even a thing. With his permed hair and chill vibes, he turned TV screens into happy little worlds full of "happy accidents." From "The Joy of Painting" to internet legend, Bob's serene landscapes now inspire countless memes and giggles.

Artist Statement

"I paint happy little memes because life needs more joy and laughter. Whether it's a tree or a cloud, there's always room for a meme-tastic twist. Remember, there are no mistakes, just meme-worthy moments waiting to happen."
